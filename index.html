Education

I did not take a classic path into academia or even realize that becoming an astrophysicist was a potential profession growing up. After growing up in the San Francisco Bay Area, I left high school early after studying abroad in Sardegna, Italy. I made my way through community college and ended up transferring to UC Berkeley. These experiences have illuminated how the field of astrophysics oftentimes presents itself as an unreachable field for many, including for myself. I strive not only to succeed in science but also help others to do the same despite many obstacles!
